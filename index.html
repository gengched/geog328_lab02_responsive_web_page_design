The French Defense is my favorite opening as black against 1.e4. It will usually lead to a complicated middlegame in which both sides have equal chances.

advantage: solid structure
disadvantage: limited space
The black pawn at e6 blocks the black's light squared bishop.

3.Nc3 3.e5 3.ed
